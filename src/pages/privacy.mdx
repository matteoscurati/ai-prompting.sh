# Privacy

_Last updated: 2026-05-07._

This site uses **Umami Cloud** (EU region) for privacy-friendly analytics.
No cookies, no fingerprinting, no cross-site tracking, no advertising. You
do not need to accept anything to read this site.

## Data controller

The site is operated by **Matteo Scurati**
([matteo.scurati@gmail.com](mailto:matteo.scurati@gmail.com)). For privacy
questions or to exercise your GDPR rights, write to that address.

## What is collected, and why

When you visit a page, the analytics script reports a single anonymous
event with:

- the URL of the page you visited
- the referring URL, if any
- your browser and operating system family (e.g. "Chrome on macOS")
- your country, resolved from IP and then discarded
- a screen size category (mobile / tablet / desktop)

To deduplicate visits within a day, Umami generates a hash from your IP
address, user agent, and a salt that **rotates every 24 hours**, then
**discards the IP**. The hash cannot be reversed to identify you and
resets daily.

This data is used to understand which docs and examples are useful, so
they can be improved.

**Legal basis:** legitimate interest under Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. The
processing is minimal, anonymized, and reasonably expected of a public
website.

## Where the data is stored

Analytics are processed by **Umami Software, Inc.** (Delaware, USA) in
their **EU region** (Frankfurt, Germany). EU customer data does not leave
the EU under their region selection. A Data Processing Agreement is in
place between us and Umami.

## What this site does not do

- No cookies are set by this site or by the analytics script.
- No fingerprinting (canvas, WebGL, font enumeration, audio context).
- No cross-site or cross-device tracking.
- No advertising networks. No third-party trackers besides Umami.
- No data is sold, rented, or shared with marketing partners.

## Hosting

The site is served by **Cloudflare Pages** (Cloudflare, Inc., USA).
Cloudflare may process minimal request metadata (IP, user agent,
timestamp) for security, caching, and rate limiting. See [Cloudflare's
privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## External links

External links to GitHub, npm, and similar may set their own cookies
once you click through. That happens on those sites, after you leave
this one. Their privacy policies apply there.

## Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port,
and object to the processing of your personal data, and to lodge a
complaint with the [Garante per la protezione dei dati
personali](https://www.gpdp.it/). Write to the email above to exercise
any of these rights. Note that anonymized analytics data cannot be
linked back to you, so erasure requests on a specific past visit are
technically infeasible.

## Opt out

You can opt out of analytics by:

- using a content blocker (uBlock Origin, Brave Shields, Pi-hole) that
  blocks `analytics.ai-prompting.sh`
- not visiting the site

## Changes

If data collection changes, this page is updated and the date at the
top reflects the change.
